Who Are They?

The Knowledge Fight podcast is hosted by the dynamic duo of Dan and Jordan, affectionately known as JorDan. They've been fact checking Alex Jones for years, and have accumulated over 1,000 episodes debunking all of the claims made on InfoWars.

Dan is the engine of the podcast. He dutifully sifts through every Alex Jones episode on InfoWars and pulls any relevant clips from what Alex is talking about on the show. On top of that, Dan researches the original source that Alex is using to make his claims, and then presents what the actual facts are.

Jordan is the voice of the listener. He hasn't heard the clips that Dan has put together, so he acts as the reactionary voice to Dan's facts.

Why Do They Do It?

From their website they describe their intentions as:

Dan and Jordan started this podcast due to a belief that fighting against his deeply flawed philosophies through debate is a fool's errand. They believe that mocking and documenting are far more powerful tools, and as such, that's what the podcast aims to do.

Sure, a lot of the subject matter is heavy, but give the show a chance, and see if you don't love it. Bet you will.
