Alex Jones is a fraud. The Knowledge Fight podcast has been fact checking Jones for years, and have accumulated hundreds of episodeds debunking all of the claims made on InfoWars. Dan and Jordan also outline just how Jones pulls off his grift by detailing all of the rhetorical tricks the large-necked host uses to con his listeners.

While this podcast is both enjoyable and informative, jumping into it can be daunting due to the overwhelming amount of information involved. This website was created to give new listeners a way to understand and jump into the Knowledge Fight podcast world.

Important Episodes

A good place to start is with some important episodes in the Knowledge Fight world. Some of these explain certain characters in InfoWars lore, while others show how certain important events were covered. Either way, these should give you a good idea of what the podcast is about.

Sandy Hook Trial Depositions

Alex Jones often says that he had little to no involvment in the ongoing harassment of the grieving Sandy Hook families. But when faced with an actual court setting, does he change his tune? Spoiler: yes he does.

9/11

Alex Jones is supposedly known for predicting 9/11. Did he actually do so, or does history remember it differently?

Debunking the Endgame Documentary

One of Alex's more famous works is his documentary "Endgame: Blueprint for Global Enslavement" that attempts to argue that a secret New World Order is attempting to achieve global control and enslavement. Dan and Jordan compare Jones's claims to actual facts, and you'll never guess what happens.

Debunking the Obama Deception Documentary

Another "documentary" by Alex Jones is his "The Obama Deception: The Mask Comes Off" film. As with the Endgame documentary, this one is filled with misinformation and straight-up lies. The KF duo break down all of them in this series.

Funny

Alex Jones either doesn't understand humor or refuses to participate in it because it doesn't make him money. But on occasion he unintentionally does something that is legitimately funny.
